About us

Who We Are & What We Stand For

Petrosa Farms is a proudly Nigerian agricultural business that specializes in both crop cultivation and livestock farming. We grow a variety of produce including fruits, vegetables, and grains, while also raising healthy livestock such as cattle, goats, and poultry. Our approach combines traditional farming values with modern techniques to ensure quality, sustainability, and food security.

From the early hours of the morning to the last light of the day, our team works tirelessly to care for the farm and the animals. We believe in transparency, ethical farming practices, and delivering fresh, farm-to-table products to homes and markets across the country.

More than just a farm, Petrosa is a vision a commitment to nourishing lives, empowering communities, and building a future where agriculture thrives. Whether you're looking for fresh produce, quality meat, or a trusted farming partner, you can count on us.

What We Do

Farming & Livestock

We grow crops like maize, rice, and tomatoes, and raise animals such as poultry, fish, and cattle to support food security.

Food Processing

We transform raw farm produce into finished products like garri, flour, palm oil, and frozen meat, adding value to local harvests.

Sales & Distribution

We buy and sell agricultural goods, tools, fertilizers, and equipment serving both local and international markets.

Training & Consulting

We provide expert training and guidance to farmers and agribusinesses on best practices and sustainable agriculture.

Storage & Logistics

We build and manage silos, cold rooms, and logistics systems to store and deliver farm products efficiently.

Smart Farming (AgriTech)

We use drones, sensors, AI, and mobile apps to monitor crops, boost productivity, and automate agricultural processes.

Farm Tourism

We create farm-based resorts and experiences where visitors can relax, learn about agriculture, and enjoy rural life.

Support Services

We raise funding, build partnerships, and work with governments and organizations to strengthen the agricultural ecosystem and empower local farmers.

Community Empowerment

Farmer Integration & Marketplace

At Petrosa Agrotech Farms, our mission goes beyond farming we're developing a platform to empower local farmers and ensure reliable access to high-quality produce. Through our digital platform, farmers can share real-time updates on their harvests, giving us a clear view of what's available across the network. We verify and approve every product before it reaches our customers, maintaining our promise of freshness, quality, and transparency.

Whether grown on our farms or sourced from trusted partners, every product listed is backed by Petrosa's commitment to excellence. We manage the entire distribution and delivery process, ensuring that only the best produce reaches wholesalers and end users. This model not only supports small-scale farmers and reduces waste but also bridges the gap between production and purchase from farm to table while strengthening our agricultural ecosystem.
