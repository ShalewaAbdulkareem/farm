About us

Who We Are & What We Stand For

Petrosa Farms is a proudly Nigerian agricultural business that specializes in both crop cultivation and livestock farming. We grow a variety of produce including fruits, vegetables, and grains, while also raising healthy livestock such as cattle, goats, and poultry. Our approach combines traditional farming values with modern techniques to ensure quality, sustainability, and food security.

From the early hours of the morning to the last light of the day, our team works tirelessly to care for the farm and the animals. We believe in transparency, ethical farming practices, and delivering fresh, farm-to-table products to homes and markets across the country.

More than just a farm, Petrosa is a vision a commitment to nourishing lives, empowering communities, and building a future where agriculture thrives. Whether you're looking for fresh produce, quality meat, or a trusted farming partner, you can count on us.

What we do

Our Produce Is Mainstay For Us

At Farm, we proudly grow a wide range of crops and raise livestock with care and attention to quality. From fresh fruits and vegetables to premium meats, eggs, and dairy, we provide locally-sourced, sustainably farmed products to nourish your family. Our goal is to bring the finest farm produce straight to your table while supporting environmentally friendly practices.

Fresh Fruit & Vegetables

We grow a variety of seasonal fruits and vegetables, including tomatoes, peppers, cucumbers, and leafy greens. Our produce is harvested at the peak of freshness and packed with nutrients, perfect for every meal.

Meat & Eggs

Our livestock is raised with care and respect, providing high-quality meat and fresh eggs. From free-range chicken to tender cuts of beef, we offer protein-rich options for your family’s nutrition.

Milk & Cheese

Our dairy products are a staple in many homes. Fresh milk, creamy cheese, and yogurt are made from cows raised on our farm, ensuring that you get the best in taste and quality.

Rice & Corn

We cultivate staple crops like rice and corn, which are carefully harvested and processed. Our grains are a perfect addition to your meals, offering quality and flavor you can rely on.
